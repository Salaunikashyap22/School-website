Different Types Of Dance Facilities In Our School

Kathakli Dance:-The first Kathakali performance was a cycle of eight stories based on the Ramayana written by one of the chieftains of a district known as Kottarakkara. The performance for each story was designed to last for six to eight hours. These stories came to be known as Ramanattom (play pertaining to Rama), which later came to be called Kathakali. Stories based on other epics and puranas were added to its repertoire in later period. ‘Kathakali’, an important genre in the Indian classical dance form, is associated with storytelling form of this art. It is the dance drama from the south Indian state of Kerala. Similar to other Indian classical dance arts, the story in ‘Kathakali’ is also communicated to audience through excellent footwork and impressive gestures of face and hands complimented with music and vocal performance.

Garba Dance:-The Garba dance is typically performed in a circle, with the participants moving around a central point called the “dandiya stick.” The movements of the dance are fast and energetic and are accompanied by the rhythmic beating of dhol drums. The dance is often performed in colourful traditional dress, with men wearing Kediyu and women wearing Chaniya choli or ghagra choli. Garba Dance is located in Malad, Mumbai and offers traditional Gujarati Garba dance classes for your sangeet nights. They have a team that can make you deliver a performance that will surprise all your guests and will make the event a place filled with fun and music. At Soni's School of Garba Dance, there is a team who has a quick hand in the Gujarati music and makes sure to bring the required energy and fun to your stage performances.

Ghoomar Dance:-The dance is chiefly performed by veiled women who wear flowing dresses called ghaghara. The dance typically involves performers pirouetting while moving in and out of a wide circle. The word ghoomna describes the twirling movement of the dancers and is the basis of the word ghoomar. Ghoomar, a traditional Bhil tribe folk dance is not just an act of singing and dancing, but is a symbol of womanhood, a rite of passage in which young girls participate to declare that they are now stepping into the shoes of a woman.What are the props for Ghoomar dance? Ghoomar dance is a form of worship that is performed on special occasions such as weddings. As accompaniments, drums, manjira, metal plates, Dhol, clash cymbals, and a harmonium are used.
Punjabi Dance:-The dance is chiefly performed by veiled women who wear flowing dresses called ghaghara. The dance typically involves performers pirouetting while moving in and out of a wide circle.
